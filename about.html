Storyline

The events of Bannerlord take place 200 years before the start of Warband, witnessing the last years of the Calradic Empire and the rise of the kingdoms from which Swadians, Rhodoks, Nords, Khergits, Vaegirs and Sarranids all claim descent.

Factions

The factions present in Bannerlord are ancestral to those of Warband, and have some of their descendant factions' traits. Additionally there will be lesser, minor factions akin to bandits and mercenaries for the player to ally with in their exploration of Imperial Calradia.

Changes from Warband

Taleworlds claims there will be "many exciting and highly requested new features" and that it will include "up-close and personal medieval combat on a huge scale, bigger, bloodier and more intense than ever before". This affirmed that they will implement what the fans want in Mount&Blade II: Bannerlord. Taleworlds are going to be incorporating aspects from mods, such as the Diplomacy mod to enhance gameplay.
